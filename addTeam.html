Adding a Team Member to a Trello Board

Purpose

The purpose of this instruction is to show how to add team members to your Trello board.

Step-By-Step Instructions

  1. Login to https://trello.com
  2. Scroll to your team name
  3. Click Members
  4. On the left of the page, click Add Members
  5. In the input field type the email address of your team member and select your team member found

    6. Note: You can look up a user by name but easier to find using email address

  6. Here is what you will see once team member added

Congratulations! You successfully added a team member.
